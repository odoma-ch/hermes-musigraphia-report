    4. 🔗 Endpoint: https://data.gesis.org/gesiskg/sparql

    5. EHRI - KG — A Linked Open Data representation of the EHRI Portal's metadata about Holocaust-related archival sources. It transforms archival descriptions—countries, institutions, collection records—into a semantically enriched, interlinked graph.

    8. 🔗 Endpoint: https://artresearch.net/sparql

    9. LinkedMusic — A federated graph of 14 music databases, including The Global Jukebox, Dig That Lick, The Sessions, RISM, MusicBrainz, and more.
